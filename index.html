The Taming of the Shrew - Pre-read notes

Need to know

  • It is a play inside of a play
  • It's meant to be a play so read it like a script
  • It takes place during the 1590s
  • Woman were meant to be submissive during the time it was written

Key vocabulary

  • thy/thee/thou/thine - forms of you
  • Shrew - A woman who is bad tempered or agressive

Character descriptions

Katherine

A beautiful but hateful and mean spirited woman who has nothing good to say to or about anyone or anything

Petruchio

A self-confident and witty man who loves a challenge and wants to marry for money

Bianca

A beautiful, sweet, docile young woman who loves music and poetry and whose father dotes on her

Lucentio

A serious scholar who sees and falls in love-at-first-sight with a beautiful young woman
